WHIT'S FROZEN CUSTARD OF FORT MILL

September 01, 2020/ Alex Trujillo

Whit's Frozen Custard Fort Mill serves up premium frozen custard made fresh daily. We are located just across from the entrance to Baxter Village. We are open 7 days a week offering 4 fresh flavors daily (Vanilla, Chocolate, Flavor of the Week and Flavor of the Day) that can be scooped in cups or cones. We also stock our most popular flavors in our Grab and Go Freezer in Pint and Quart containers. We also offer shakes, sundaes, banana splits and Whitsers (any of our array of toppings lightly blended with one of the freshly made flavors). Our most popular item is the Whittie. It's our custard sandwiched between two store-baked cookies. We typically have a dozen or more varieties available for purchase. We also have made to order custard cakes and are available to cater any size event.

WHIT’S FROZEN CUSTARD OF CLINTONVILLE

December 18, 2019/ Alex Trujillo

With our home ties to Granville and having lived in Columbus since attending college at OSU – I wanted to continue to serve Whit’s Frozen Custard in the neighborhoods I have called home for the last 20 years. With the Short North in place, it was time to expand to my home turf – Clintonville. After looking for a couple years, we feel we have found the perfect location and fit to finally serve up some Whit’s Frozen Custard made fresh daily. We already had a nice following from Weiland's Market and Lucky's Market as well as our novelty Whittie Sandwiches from Studio 35 in Clintonville. Now I can’t wait to walk to work and serve it fresh too! Now serving Vegan Desserts made with cultured coconut milk! At the Clintonville location we are proud to serve the Vegan community with a freshly made daily vanilla as well as a weekly flavor. We have expanded our Vegan options to include a variety of toppings, shakes made with Flax milk, vegan frozen cakes (pre-made or special order), as well as a fully dedicated grab-n-go with vegan pints/quart flavors and our famous Vegan Whittie.
